About Techmasters



Yup. We have a website.
Techmasters is a student run organization at Phillips Academy Andover that is oriented around the world of technology.

Techmasters provide students with free IT and software help, as Techmasters is for the students - by the students. At the beginning of each school year, Techmasters are available to help install and set-up laptops, printers, and other devices for new or returning students.

Besides Techmasters' IT background, Techmasters is also a club that meets every week in the Gelb science building in room 202 at 1:30 pm. There, members would conduct meetings on computers and technology and provide instruction for students interested in learning more about these topics, whether they are coding, graphics design, etc. If you would like to participate, feel free to contact us by heading to the Contact Us page here.
