Techmasters is an on-campus tech support organization.
At the same time, we are a computer security club.

Techmasters has been in existence since at least 1996 as a tech support organization, assigned with installing Ethernet across campus. Today, Techmasters has also become a club for anybody interested in technology and computer security. We normally meet for an hour every Sunday to discuss topics such as ethical hacking, networking, programming, game design, and whatnot. Techmasters is great if you want to learn and discuss modern tech with people just like you. All is welcome regardless of how good you are at technology. It's a terabyte of fun!
