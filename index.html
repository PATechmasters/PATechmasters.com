Techmasters is an on-campus tech help organization. We're also a club if you are interested in all things tech.

Techmasters originally started out in 1998 as an on-campus tech help organization, assigned with installing Ethernet across campus. Fast forward to 2016, where Techmasters has also become a club for anybody interested in technology. We normally meet for an hour on Sundays to talk about topics such as gadgets, programming, game design, and whatnot. Techmasters is great if you want to learn and discuss modern tech with people just like you. Ultimately, you should become a Techmaster regardless of how good you are at technology. It's a terabyte of fun!
