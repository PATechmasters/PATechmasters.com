Getting Help

As a Phillips Academy student, there are many ways to receive Technical assistance, so do not be afraid to ask.

You can get one-on-one help with a Techmaster at anytime between 6:00 PM and 8:00 PM on Mondays and Thursdays on the second floor of the library. Please bring your charger, PAnet login credentials, and computer login credentials, and we'll see if we can fix your problem. If that doesn't work for you, you can also use the live chat, send an email to techmasters@andover.edu, or check out the FAQs below.

FAQS

Frequently asked questions

PANet / Canvas

How do I change my password?

Go to mypassword.andover.edu and follow the directions there

I forgot my password. Help!

To reset your password, head to mypassword.andover.edu and answer your security questions. If you forgot both your password and security answers, you will need to contact the OIT help desk located in the PACC (basement of the library)

I can't log in to Canvas

Try going to PAnet, relogging in, and then clicking the link to Canvas. If this doesn't work, clear your cookies (Clear browser history and check "cookies")

Printers

My wireless printer won't work

Andover's WiFi runs on a different security protocol than what wireless printers are designed to run on, so your printer will not work on Andover's network.

My wired printer won't work

This is most likely due to the correct drivers being installed. Install the driver included with the printer (A disk or a link to a website was most likely included with the printer), and the printer should work.

My browser can't load PrintOn

You must be connected to PA's Wifi to use Andover's PrintOn service

WiFi

How do I connect to Andover WiFi?

Find "PASTUDENT" in your computer's network list. Input the same credentials you use to log in to PAnet and Canvas.

Why is my WiFi so slow?

This is because there are so many students on the internet.
