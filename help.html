Getting Help



As a Phillips Academy student, there are several ways to receive Technical assistance.

You can stop by the second floor of the OWHL to receive one-on-one help with a Techmaster any time between 6:00 PM and 8:00 PM on Mondays and Thursdays. Please bring your charger, PAnet login credentials, and computer login credentials. We'll see if we can fix your problem (we probably could).

If you need more immediate assistance, these times don't work, or you just have a question for us, you can send an email to the Techmasters board through techmasters@andover.edu.

Of course, you can always use our live chat system right here on this website located near the bottom right of your internet browser's window.
